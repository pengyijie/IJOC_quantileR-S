In the Zip file named quantile R&S, we can find the online appendix and codes associated with a paper titled as ``Efficient Sampling Allocation Procedures for Optimal Quantile Selection'' authored by Yijie Peng, Chun-Hung Chen, Michael C. Fu, Jian-Qiang Hu, and Ilya O. Ryzhov. 

The codes written in Matlab m files are implementations of the numerical examples The descriptions about the setting of the numerical experiments can be found in the paper and online appendix.
